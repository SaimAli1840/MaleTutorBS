About Us

Welcome! Male tutor expert is an online tutoring platform offering Math group classes across KS3, GCSE, IGCSE and A-level. Our founder of Male Tutor Expert, Shabiib Oyewole has delivered over 1500 lessons through 1-1 support, group classes in centres and online since 2016. He has also endured an unique journey in his Mathematics from primary school holding the timetables record to achieving a Bachelors 2:1 in Mathematics and Statistics during the Covid Pandemic.

It is our mission to ensure every student with us reaches their peak skillset and mindset in preperation for exams and seek greater opportunities for further study or employment with Mathematics. Outside of online tutoring, He enjoys travelling, languages, Brazilian Jiu Jutsu and food!
